About Me

Hi, I'm Sai Keerthan M, a passionate Prompt Engineer and Generative AI enthusiast with over 3 years of professional experience in software engineering, AI tool development, and autonomous systems. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Rivier University and a Bachelor’s from Kakatiya University.

I started my career at Cognizant, where I worked on testing and validating ML-based lane detection systems for autonomous vehicles. My work involved high-accuracy annotation across various visual formats, tool validation, and mentoring new team members.

Driven by a deep interest in AI automation and NLP, I transitioned into the Generative AI space. At Conflux Systems, I’ve been developing prompt strategies, enhancing chatbot accuracy, and leading A/B tests to improve LLM-driven conversational systems. My core expertise lies in LLM prompting techniques like Chain-of-Thought, Zero-shot, and Mega prompting, along with tools like OpenAI, LangChain, and Bolt.ai.

Notable projects include ResumeBuddy, a resume-generating chatbot with 95% job-matching accuracy, and a real-time facial emotion tracking system using OpenCV and MediaPipe. I also authored a research paper on Human-Computer Interaction using AI, focusing on smart assistants and contextual communication.

I actively explore tools like Gemini, DALLE, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and Eleven Labs to bring creative and intelligent solutions to life. I'm always eager to collaborate on projects that blend human-centered design with cutting-edge AI.
